WHY JCC: Beyond a Conference Experience

Discover Jokey's Conference Center (JCC), an exceptional venue strategically situated in the heart of Jakarta. More than just a Conference center, JCC has been a trusted host to influential gatherings of global leaders in politics, business, and entertainment. With over 30 years of experience, our dedicated staff possesses a diverse and extensive portfolio, allowing us to cater to the unique needs of our valued clients.

We have successfully organized and executed over 10,000 events, ranging from world Conferences and regional conferences to captivating theatrical performances, vibrant jazz festivals, electrifying concerts, dazzling fashion shows, impactful product launches, productive business meetings, and exquisite banquets. Our esteemed clientele includes presidents, CEOs, renowned international musicians, and companies from diverse industries.

At JCC, we offer an all-encompassing event experience, with a wide range of facilities conveniently located under one roof. Our expansive exhibition, event, and meeting spaces are equipped with cutting-edge sound, lighting, and communication systems. Ethernet access is available throughout the venue, ensuring seamless connectivity for your event.

To assist you in designing and orchestrating your event, our dedicated Conference and Exhibition Services Managers are committed to guiding you every step of the way. From exquisite flower arrangements to meticulous lighting management and comprehensive contingency planning, our team ensures that every detail and requirement is met with utmost precision and professionalism.

Choose JCC for an extraordinary event experience that goes beyond expectations. Let us transform your vision into a remarkable reality, creating memorable moments that will leave a lasting impression on your attendees.
